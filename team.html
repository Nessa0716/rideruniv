Our Team

Meet the dedicated individuals behind our brand. Our team is passionate about fitness, student engagement, and creating a unique apparel line that represents the gym culture on campus. Each member brings a unique set of skills and experiences, making this brand a reality for students who take pride in their fitness journey.

Louis Ogno

Louis Ogno is the CEO and founder of the brand. As a finance major, he combines his business expertise with his passion for fitness and sports. He enjoys outdoor activities on campus and believes in fostering a strong fitness community.

The idea for this brand was born from his observation that many students love working out at the campus gym but lack a unique, signature brand to represent their community. Instead of wearing mainstream fitness brands like Gymshark, he wanted to create something meaningful that students could proudly wear.

Alisha Brown

Alisha Brown is a Human Resource Management major and the Chief People Officer (CPO) of the brand. She is passionate about fostering a sense of belonging within the student community.

Alisha is deeply involved in campus activities and believes in giving back. Her decision to join the brand stemmed from her desire to create a movement that represents students who are committed to fitness and overall wellness.

Oscar Brajczewski

Oscar Brajczewski is a freshman majoring in finance and serves as the Chief Financial Officer (CFO) of the brand. He is also the treasurer of the men’s club soccer team, which has strengthened his leadership and financial management skills.

With his love for soccer and fitness, Oscar understands the importance of team spirit and dedication. His role in the brand involves handling financial decisions to ensure the growth and sustainability of the apparel line, while also connecting with students through athletics.

Glenroy Heron

Glenroy Heron is a Sports Management major and the Chief Marketing Officer (CMO) of the brand. His enthusiasm for athletics and student engagement drives his innovative marketing strategies.

Excited about the opportunity to promote fitness culture on campus, Glenroy is working on developing marketing campaigns that will engage students and build a strong brand presence. He is passionate about using creative events and social media to spread the brand’s message.

Brianna Prado

Brianna Prado is a Healthcare Management major and the Chief Operating Officer (COO) of the brand. She plays a crucial role in ensuring the brand’s mission aligns with promoting health and wellness on campus.

With a passion for fitness and student well-being, Brianna focuses on executing strategies that bring the brand closer to students. She believes in the power of fitness apparel to inspire and motivate students to prioritize their health.
